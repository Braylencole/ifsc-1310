Thoughts on Progressive Enhancement

Progressive Enhancement might seem like a regular thing now, but it's actually had quite the journey to get here. Back in the day, there was this other concept called graceful degradation. It was all about making sure a website at least kinda worked, even if it wasn't at its best. But then along came Progressive Enhancement, which changed the game. Instead of just making things passable, it focused on building a solid foundation that worked for everyone, no matter what device or browser they were using.
